Our State-of-the-Art Facility

Modern Laundry Equipment & Community Space

Liberty Laundry is a veteran-owned business dedicated to providing the Westover community with top-quality laundry facilities. Our facility features 22 state-of-the-art washing machines and dryers, ranging from 20 to 80 pounds in capacity.

We've invested in the latest equipment to ensure your clothes are cleaned with precision and care. Our fully attended facility means you always have assistance when you need it. We accept both cash and mobile app payments for your convenience.

Beyond clean laundry, we offer a welcoming community space with a snack bar and a fresh Mexican food truck in our parking lot (closed Mondays). Whether you're doing a quick wash or settling in for the day, Liberty Laundry is your neighborhood destination.
